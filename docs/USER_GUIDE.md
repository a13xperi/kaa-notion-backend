## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Complete the Intake Form**: Start by filling out our intake form with details about your project, including:
   - Property address
   - Project type (residential, commercial, landscape, etc.)
   - Budget range
   - Timeline preferences
   - Existing documents (surveys, drawings)

2. **Review Your Tier Recommendation**: Based on your project details, we'll recommend the best service tier for your needs:
   - **Tier 1 (Seedling)**: Basic concept design
   - **Tier 2 (Sprout)**: Detailed design with revisions
   - **Tier 3 (Canopy)**: Full-service design package

3. **Complete Payment**: After selecting your tier, complete payment through our secure Stripe checkout.

4. **Access Your Portal**: Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive an email with login credentials to access your client portal.

### First Login

1. Visit the portal at `https://portal.sage-platform.com`
2. Enter your email and password
3. You'll be taken directly to your project dashboard

## Milestones & Progress

### Understanding Milestones

Your project is divided into milestones, which are key phases of work:

**Tier 1 (Seedling)**:
1. Intake & Site Analysis
2. Concept Development
3. Final Delivery

**Tier 2 (Sprout)**:
1. Intake & Discovery
2. Initial Draft
3. Client Review
4. Revisions
5. Final Package

**Tier 3 (Canopy)**:
1. Comprehensive Intake
2. Site Analysis
3. Concept Options
4. Design Development
5. Client Presentation
6. Revisions
7. Final Documentation

### Milestone Statuses

- **Pending**: Not yet started
- **In Progress**: Currently being worked on
- **Completed**: Finished
- **Blocked**: Waiting for your input

### Timeline View

The milestone timeline shows:
- Completed milestones (green checkmarks)
- Current milestone (highlighted)
- Upcoming milestones (gray)
- Expected completion dates

## Deliverables

### What Are Deliverables?

Deliverables are the files and documents we create for your project:
- Concept designs
- Draft drawings
- Final plans
- Reference materials
- Presentation documents

### Viewing Deliverables

1. Navigate to your project
2. Scroll to the **Deliverables** section
3. See all available files organized by category

### Categories

- **Concept**: Initial design concepts
- **Draft**: Work-in-progress designs
- **Final**: Completed, approved designs
- **Reference**: Supporting materials
- **Other**: Additional documents

### Downloading Files

1. Find the file you want to download
2. Click the **Download** button
3. The file will download to your device

Files are available in their original format (PDF, CAD, images, etc.).

### File Preview

For images and PDFs, you can preview files directly in the browser before downloading.

## Revision Requests

### Requesting Changes

If you need changes to a deliverable:

1. Go to the relevant milestone
2. Click **Request Revision**
3. Describe the changes you need
4. Set a priority level:
   - **Low**: Nice to have, not urgent
   - **Medium**: Important, address in normal workflow
   - **High**: Critical, needs immediate attention
5. Submit your request

### Revision Limits

Your service tier includes a set number of revisions:
- **Tier 1**: 1 round of minor revisions
- **Tier 2**: 2 rounds of revisions
- **Tier 3**: 3 rounds of comprehensive revisions

Additional revisions may incur extra charges.

### Tracking Revisions

View all revision requests in the **Revisions** tab:
- **Pending**: Submitted, awaiting response
- **In Progress**: Being worked on
- **Completed**: Changes made
- **Rejected**: Not feasible (with explanation)

## Notifications

### Staying Updated

The notification system keeps you informed about:
- New deliverables uploaded
- Milestone completions
- Message responses
- Payment confirmations
- Revision updates

### Notification Center

1. Click the bell icon in the header
2. View all recent notifications
3. Click a notification to go to the relevant page
4. Mark notifications as read

### Push Notifications

Enable push notifications to receive alerts even when you're not on the site:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Click **Enable Push Notifications**
3. Allow notifications in your browser
4. You'll now receive real-time alerts

### Managing Notifications

- **Mark as Read**: Click the notification or use the mark all as read button
- **Delete**: Remove notifications you no longer need
- **Settings**: Customize which notifications you receive

## Mobile App

### Installing the App

SAGE is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can install it like a native app:

**On iPhone/iPad:**
1. Open Safari
2. Go to `https://portal.sage-platform.com`
3. Tap the Share button
4. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
5. Name it "SAGE" and tap Add

**On Android:**
1. Open Chrome
2. Go to `https://portal.sage-platform.com`
3. Tap the menu (three dots)
4. Tap **Add to Home Screen** or **Install App**
5. Confirm the installation

### Offline Access

The app works offline too:
- View your project information
- Browse cached deliverables
- Messages will sync when you're back online

## FAQ

### General Questions

**How long does a typical project take?**
- Tier 1: 1-2 weeks
- Tier 2: 2-4 weeks
- Tier 3: 4-8 weeks

Actual timelines depend on project complexity and your responsiveness to review requests.

**Can I upgrade my tier mid-project?**
Yes, contact us to discuss upgrading your service tier. We'll prorate the difference.

**What file formats are deliverables provided in?**
- Design drawings: PDF and CAD formats
- Images: PNG, JPG, or PDF
- Documents: PDF

### Technical Issues

**I can't log in. What should I do?**
1. Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link
2. Check your email spam folder for reset emails
3. Contact support if issues persist

**Files won't download. What's wrong?**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser
3. Disable any download-blocking extensions
4. Contact support if issues persist

**The app isn't working on my phone.**
1. Ensure you're using a modern browser (Safari, Chrome)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try reinstalling the PWA
4. Contact support if issues persist

### Billing Questions

**How do I view my payment history?**
Go to Settings > Billing to see all past payments and invoices.

**Can I get a refund?**
Please review our refund policy or contact support to discuss your specific situation.

**How do I update my payment method?**
Payment updates are handled through Stripe. Contact support for assistance.
